Understanding the Signals

VERIFIED

High confidence. The claim is supported by multiple authoritative sources (e.g., gov, edu, major news).

REPUTABLE

Good quality. The source is generally trustworthy, though the specific claim might be opinion or complex.

UNSCORED

Caution advised. Insufficient data, entertainment content, or sources with mixed factual history.
